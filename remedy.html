Leaf Smut

Caused By:

Leaf smut, caused by the fungus Entyloma oryzae, is awidely distributed, but somewhat minor, disease of rice

Remedy:

Remove and destroy any affected part of the infected plant.Spray of propiconazole and hexaconazole,effective in managing the rice false smut

Brown Spot

Caused By:

Cochliobolus miyabeanus (formerly known as Helminthosporium oryzae) is a fungus that causes brown spot disease.

Remedy:

In order to control this disease, three phenolic antioxidants were tested (salicylic acid, benzoic acid and hydroquinone).

Bacterial Leaf Blight

Caused By:

Bacterial leaf blight of rice (BB) is a disease caused by the Gram-negative bacterium Xanthomonas oryzae pv. oryzae (Xoo),

Remedy:

Terramycin 17, Brestanol, Agrimycin 500 and a combination of Agrimycin 100 + Fytolan gave effective control of the blight phase of the disease. The combination of Agrimycin 100 + Fytolan spraying gave good control of the disease
